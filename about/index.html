About

This website is an information website about TSA Chapter and CTE Program at our school. Our website also contains our research about Artificial Intelligence . It is written with HTML, CSS and jQuery. This site is built with Jekyll, which is a a static website generator. Our website is hosted on Github. Our team used Media Queries in order to make the website responsive. We benefited from Material Design, which made our website more effective and colorful.. All photos used in this website are either in the public domain or taken by our members.
