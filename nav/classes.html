Arduino Classes

The students learn how to use microcontrollers and embedded systems engineering with the help of the Arduino classes taught by Prof. Dr. Mundt, which are organized by the German Department.

IEL Robotics

The Robotics Club of our school grows by means of the classes given by the club members to junior participants, which cover Introduction to Arduino, C++ Libraries and their Utilization Introduction to Electronics and Making of Printed Circuits. The aim of the club classes is that new members reach the level of ability to help with on-going projects by the end of the school year.

Science Olympiad Classes

Istanbul Lisesi has founded the Science Olympiad club, which is shortened as ILOG (Istanbul Lisesi Olympiad Group), comprising of mathematics, informatics, and biology contestants. The courses are given by experienced teachers and especially in informatics and biology, older students hold additional lectures for newcomers as a tradition.
