Jugend Forscht

Jugendforscht is a project competition sponsored by the Federal Ministry of Education and Research in Germany to support talented young people aged 15-21 in Mathematics, Programming, Natural Sciences and Technical fields.In the Jugend Forscht Science Competition held in Thuringia, Germany, Istanbul Lisesi students won 1 Gold and 4 Bronze medals and achieved great success. In the IEL Robotics Club, while the project entitled "T-shirt blocking hunching" was awarded a gold medal, the projects in the field of Biology were awarded with bronze medals.
